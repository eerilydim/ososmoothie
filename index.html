Superlative superfood smoothies, soups, and healthy sweet treats for everybody! Everything we make is dairy-free, gluten-free, and vegan-friendly to boot! Our number 1 priority is creating delicious smoothies that are also powerfully nutritious.

The menu includes a range of rich and creamy to light and refreshing smoothies. There will be everyday smoothies and a specials board that changes from week to week.

We have low-carb options for those who wish to watch their sugar intake and high protein and energy shakes for those looking to power through the day. All smoothies will be made from fresh and healthy ingredients sourced and bought locally where possible.
